Spinning Stories is a storytelling game featuring tales and drawings of the Knights Park Bridge. Authors from St John's Primary School, Bedelsford School, The Bradbury Centre and Kingston School of Art all contributed to a collection of stories inspired by their experiences with the Knights Park Bridge. The spinner then jumbles them up to create new stories and drawings.

W/ Matt Clark, Louisa Murray & Marnie Boller
